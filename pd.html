Delicious Kapampangan Pindang Damulag

Background of the Recipe

Pindang Damulag is a Kapampangan dish made from fermented carabao (water buffalo) meat. Known for its tangy and savory flavor, it’s a well-loved local delicacy.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram carabao meat (damulag), sliced thinly
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup pineapple juice or calamansi juice (for curing)
  • 1 medium onion, sliced (for sautéing)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup water (for cooking)

Instructions

  1. Slice the carabao meat thinly for faster fermentation and better flavor absorption.
  2. In a bowl, combine the meat, salt, sugar, ground pepper, minced garlic, and pineapple or calamansi juice.
  3. Massage the mixture into the meat thoroughly.
  4. Transfer to a clean container or jar, cover tightly, and leave to ferment in the refrigerator for 2-3 days.
  5. Heat cooking oil in a pan and sauté the onions until translucent.
  6. Add the fermented meat and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  7. Pour in the water and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the meat is tender and the sauce has slightly thickened.
  8. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice and a side of fresh vegetables or pickled papaya (atchara).
